# Dwarf Fortress Wiki Blueprint

Reference inspiration:
- Based on the structure and visual style of the existing Project Zomboid wiki hosted under GameWikiHub.
- Goal: create a practical, readable, dark-themed survival/management knowledge hub focused on discoverability, SEO, and long-session reading.
- Target audience: new players, returning players, Steam players, fortress builders, adventure mode players, mod users, and advanced simulation players.

---

# 1. Overall Direction

## Core Theme
Dwarf Fortress should feel:
- Ancient
- Brutal
- Procedurally generated
- Dense with systems
- Simulation-heavy
- Medieval fantasy
- Legendary and chaotic

The website should feel like:
- A digital dwarven archive
- A fortress management manual
- A deep simulation encyclopedia
- A military engineering handbook

Compared to the Project Zomboid wiki:
- More parchment + stone influence
- Less modern survival UI
- More engraved textures and medieval iconography
- Slightly more fantasy aesthetic
