# 08-dwarf-fortress-wiki.md

# Dwarf Fortress Wiki Expansion Project

## Project Overview

Website:

dwarf-fortress.gamewikihub.com

Primary Goal:

Create the most useful beginner-friendly and strategy-focused Dwarf Fortress resource on the web.

The site should not function as a database.

The site should function as a veteran player explaining the game to newer players.

Google already knows item names, workshop recipes, and game mechanics.

We must provide:

- interpretation
- strategy
- recommendations
- decision making
- common mistakes
- player experience

---

# Content Philosophy

Dwarf Fortress is not a game players search for because they want raw information.

Players search because they want answers to questions like:

- Why did my fortress collapse?
- What should I build first?
- Why are my dwarves unhappy?
- How do I survive the first winter?
- Why are goblins destroying me?
- What industries should I prioritize?

The wiki should answer those questions.

---

# Content Generation Rules

Every article must include:

## Overview

What the topic is.

## Why It Matters

Why players should care.

## Common Mistakes

What beginners usually do wrong.

## Strategy Discussion

How experienced players approach the topic.

## Community Opinion

What long-time players generally recommend.

## Recommendations

Clear actionable advice.

## Related Guides

Internal links.
